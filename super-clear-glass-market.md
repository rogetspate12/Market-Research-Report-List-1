<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Super Clear Glass market is primarily categorized into Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a textured surface and varying thicknesses, often used in decorative applications. Float Glass, on the other hand, is made by floating molten glass on molten tin, creating a smooth, even surface ideal for windows and architectural applications. Both types offer high clarity and transparency, making them popular choices for various industries, including construction and design.</p></p>
