<p><p>The courier software market can be divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. Conversely, cloud-based software operates on remote servers, providing flexibility, easier updates, and scalability without the need for extensive infrastructure. Cloud solutions often use a subscription model, making them more accessible for smaller businesses, while on-premise options may cater to larger enterprises with specific needs.</p></p>
