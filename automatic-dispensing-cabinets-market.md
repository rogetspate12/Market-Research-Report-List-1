<p><p>Automatic Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) are crucial in medication management within healthcare settings. Centralized ADCs are typically located in a central pharmacy or storage area, allowing for streamlined inventory control and medication dispensing to multiple departments. In contrast, decentralized ADCs are situated closer to the point of care, such as patient wards, enabling quicker access to medications for healthcare providers. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce medication errors, and improve patient safety by ensuring accurate and timely drug delivery across healthcare facilities.</p></p>
