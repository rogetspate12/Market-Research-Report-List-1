<p><p>The liquid polysulfide polymer market is categorized into two main types: thiol end and epoxy end. Thiol end polymers feature sulfur-containing end groups that provide flexibility and enhance adhesion, making them ideal for coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Epoxy end polymers, on the other hand, exhibit superior chemical resistance and mechanical properties, making them suitable for applications requiring durability, such as protective coatings and construction materials. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
