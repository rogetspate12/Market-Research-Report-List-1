<p><strong>The High Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Temperature Detector</li><li>Thermocouple</li><li>Semiconductor-based Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature sensors market features three main types: Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), thermocouples, and semiconductor-based sensors. RTDs use the resistance change of metals to measure temperature accurately, ideal for precise applications. Thermocouples consist of two dissimilar metals joined at one end, generating a voltage proportional to temperature differences, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Semiconductor-based sensors leverage the temperature sensitivity of semiconductor materials, offering compact designs and quick response times, ideal for integrated circuits and electronics applications.</p></p>
