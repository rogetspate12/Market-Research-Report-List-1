<p><ul><li>Channel Waveguide</li><li>Planar Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Waveguide Display market features two primary types: channel waveguides and planar waveguides. Channel waveguides are structured with distinct paths for light propagation, enabling high precision and integration in compact devices, often used in telecommunications and sensor applications. In contrast, planar waveguides feature a flat design that allows for broader light distribution and simpler fabrication, making them suitable for displays and lighting applications. Both types play crucial roles in advancing display technologies and enhancing user experiences.</p></p>
