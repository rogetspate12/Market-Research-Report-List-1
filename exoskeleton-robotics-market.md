<p><p>The exoskeleton robotics market is categorized into three primary types: lower-body, upper-body, and full-body exoskeletons. Lower-body exoskeletons are designed to assist with mobility and rehabilitation, supporting the legs and spine. Upper-body exoskeletons augment arm strength and reduce fatigue, benefiting workers in physically demanding jobs or aiding rehabilitation of upper limb injuries. Full-body exoskeletons incorporate both upper and lower support, enhancing overall mobility and strength, and are often used in industrial applications, rehabilitation, and military contexts.</p></p>
