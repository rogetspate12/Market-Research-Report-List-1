<p><p>An Organic Electron Transport Layer (OETL) is a crucial component in organic electronic devices, including organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs) and organic photovoltaics (OPVs). OETLs enhance charge transport by facilitating the movement of electrons from the cathode to the emissive layer in OLEDs or from the active layer to the cathode in OPVs. Their ability to be processed through low-cost methods such as solution processing and printing has driven interest in their application for flexible and lightweight electronic devices.</p><p>The Organic Electron Transport Layer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
