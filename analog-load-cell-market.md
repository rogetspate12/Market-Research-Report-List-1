<p><p>An analog load cell is a type of transducer that converts an applied mechanical force into an electrical signal. It utilizes strain gauges to measure deformation, producing a voltage output proportional to the weight or force being applied. These load cells are widely used in various applications, including industrial weighing, material testing, and automation systems.</p><p>The Analog Load Cell Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.70% during the forecast period.
