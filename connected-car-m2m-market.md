<p><p>The Connected Car M2M market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Embedded Solutions** involve built-in connectivity hardware and software within the vehicle, enabling continuous communication without dependence on external devices. </p><p>2. **Integrated Solutions** combine embedded and mobile technologies, leveraging both on-board systems and external networks for enhanced functionality. </p><p>3. **Tethered Solutions** use smartphones or other devices to connect the vehicle to the internet, providing access to features and data through a mobile interface. Each type offers distinct benefits for vehicle communication and user experience.</p></p>
