<p><ul><li>Enteral Feeding Pumps</li><li>Enteral Feeding Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enteral Nutrition System market consists primarily of two key types: enteral feeding pumps and enteral feeding tubes. Enteral feeding pumps are medical devices that regulate the delivery of liquid nutrition directly to patients' gastrointestinal tracts, ensuring precise dosing and convenience. Enteral feeding tubes, on the other hand, are flexible tubes inserted into the stomach or intestines, providing a direct pathway for nutrition. Together, these components facilitate effective nutritional support for patients unable to consume food orally due to health conditions.</p></p>
