<p><p>The refined peanut oil market includes two primary extraction methods: cold pressed and hot pressed. Cold pressed oil is extracted at low temperatures, preserving more nutrients and flavor, making it ideal for salad dressings and gourmet cooking. In contrast, hot pressed oil is extracted using heat, which can enhance the oil's flavor and increase yield but may diminish some nutrients. Both types cater to different culinary needs, influencing consumer preferences and market demand in the refined peanut oil sector.</p></p>
