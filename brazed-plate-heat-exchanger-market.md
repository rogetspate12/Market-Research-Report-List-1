<p><ul><li>Single Circuit</li><li>Multi Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brazed Plate Heat Exchanger market is categorized into two main types: single circuit and multi circuit. Single circuit heat exchangers feature a single channel for fluid flow, suitable for straightforward heating or cooling applications, providing efficient thermal transfer. In contrast, multi circuit heat exchangers contain multiple pathways, allowing for complex processes and greater flexibility in temperature control. This versatility makes them ideal for industrial and commercial systems requiring simultaneous heating and cooling of different fluids.</p></p>
