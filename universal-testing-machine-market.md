<p><strong>The Universal Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Testing Machine (UTM) market is primarily divided into two types: Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. Electromechanical UTMs utilize electric motors and are suitable for precise measurements and control in materials testing. They are typically faster and offer high accuracy for small-scale tests. In contrast, Hydraulic UTMs use hydraulic power for testing larger materials and can exert substantial force, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct requirements based on the materials and tests involved.</p></p>
