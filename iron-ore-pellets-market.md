Blast furnace pellets are designed for use in traditional iron and steel production, wherein they are smelted at high temperatures to produce iron. In contrast, direct reduction pellets are tailored for processes that utilize lower temperatures, often in direct reduction iron (DRI) methods. Each type serves distinct metallurgical needs, catering to different production techniques within the steelmaking industry.</p></p>
