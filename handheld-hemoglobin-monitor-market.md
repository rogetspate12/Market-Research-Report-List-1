<p><strong>The Handheld Hemoglobin Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive</li><li>Non-invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld hemoglobin monitor market is divided into two main types: intrusive and non-invasive. Intrusive monitors require a blood sample to measure hemoglobin levels, offering accurate and reliable results but involving discomfort for the patient. In contrast, non-invasive monitors utilize advanced technology, such as light absorption or spectroscopy, to assess hemoglobin levels without the need for blood samples. These devices provide convenience and ease of use, appealing to patients who prefer a painless monitoring experience.</p></p>
