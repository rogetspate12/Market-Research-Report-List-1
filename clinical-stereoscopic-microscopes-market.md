Binocular stereoscopic microscopes feature two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable and natural viewing experience, ideal for detailed examinations. Trinocular stereoscopic microscopes include an additional port for a camera, allowing for photography and video capture of specimens, thus expanding research and documentation capabilities in clinical settings.</p></p>
