<p><p>VoIP phone systems are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based VoIP systems operate over the internet, allowing businesses to access communication tools without extensive hardware, reducing costs and enabling scalability. In contrast, on-premises VoIP systems require companies to invest in and maintain their own hardware and infrastructure, providing greater control but often increasing initial expenditures. Both types cater to different organizational needs, with cloud solutions favored for flexibility and on-premises systems for security.</p></p>
