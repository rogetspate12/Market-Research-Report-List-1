<p><ul><li>Earth Pressure Balancing Pipe Jacking Machine</li><li>Mud-Water Balancing Pipe Jacking Machine</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe jacking machine market is categorized into several types, including Earth Pressure Balancing (EPB) machines, Mud-Water Balancing machines, and others. EPB machines maintain stable pressure by balancing soil and groundwater, making them suitable for urban environments. Mud-Water Balancing machines utilize a mix of water and bentonite to support the excavation face, preventing collapses in unstable ground. Other types may include mixed-shield machines or microtunneling systems, each tailored for specific geological conditions and project requirements, ensuring efficient pipe installation.</p></p>
