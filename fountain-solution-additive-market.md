<p><ul><li>Acidic</li><li>Neutral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fountain solution additives are essential in the printing process, categorized into two primary market types: acidic and neutral. Acidic fountain solutions help enhance ink adhesion and maintain pH balance, ensuring optimal performance in offset printing. They are ideal for high-speed presses and environments with varying water quality. In contrast, neutral solutions offer a balanced pH that minimizes corrosion and improves plate longevity, making them suitable for sensitive substrates and eco-friendly printing practices. Each type serves distinct needs in the printing industry.</p></p>
