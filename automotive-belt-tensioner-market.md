<p><ul><li>Manual Tensioner</li><li>Automatic Tensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive belt tensioner market is divided into two main types: manual tensioners and automatic tensioners. Manual tensioners require manual adjustment to maintain the proper tension in the belt, which can be labor-intensive and less precise. In contrast, automatic tensioners use spring-loaded mechanisms to self-adjust and maintain optimal tension, ensuring better performance and reducing wear on the belt. These automatic systems offer greater convenience and efficiency, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicle designs.</p></p>
