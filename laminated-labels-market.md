<p><p>The laminated labels market is categorized into three main types based on the adhesive technologies used. Water-based laminated labels utilize environmentally friendly adhesives that offer good performance and are suitable for a variety of applications. Solvent-based laminated labels employ adhesives derived from organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, but may pose environmental concerns. UV curable laminated labels utilize ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, offering fast production times and enhanced resistance to chemicals and moisture, making them ideal for demanding applications.</p></p>
